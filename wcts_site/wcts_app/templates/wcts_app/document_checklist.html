Required Documentation To obtain the following documents, you must notify your school district of your intent to attend Warren Tech. Do not send this application until all documents are included.


Grade Seven and Eight Report Cards
PARCC Scores
Complete Middle School Discipline Record
Signed Parental Release
Letter of Recommendation from Guidance Counselor or Administrator Summarizing Student’s Overall Performance at the Middle School Level
Letter of Recommendation from Teacher or Other Individual Familiar with the Student
Essay Answering the Following Questions: Why do You Want to Attend Warren Tech? – AND – Why do you want to enroll in the career program you are choosing?

Please all of the information above to one of the following staff members.

  • Principal
  • Vice Principal
  • Administrative Secretary/Guidance
  • Freshman and Sophomore Counsler
  • Junior and Senior Counsler
